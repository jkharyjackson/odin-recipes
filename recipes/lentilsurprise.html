Lentil Surprise

Description

This recipe is a staple for those needing something quick but unwilling to settle for the high sodium of Quick Ramen.

Have a pot ready to boil water, a measuring cup, the spices listed to follow, red lentils, and the *optional* - white quinoa for protein. This should only take about 15mins.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Red Lentils
  • 1/4 White Quinoa
  • All Spice
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Red Chipotle Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cardamom
  • Garlic Seasoning or Powder

Steps

  1. Measure 1 Cup and a half - or approximately a 3:1 ratio of the desired single/combined measurement of Red Lentil and White Quinoa - of water.
  2. Bring water to a boil.
  3. Add all ingredients to boiling water. Put cover on pot and immediately lower burner setting to allow simmering. Otherwise *it will rise fast* and make a mess (pause).
  4. Check back in 15 mins. The Lentils should look soft and if you added White Quinoa they should look clear and translucent as opposed to the uncooked hard grain look.
  5. Grab a spoon.
  6. Bon Provecho!
